About us

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College is a technical education institution located in Ludhiana, Punjab, India. It is named after Guru Nanak Dev, the founder of the Sikh religion, and it has a Gurdwara Sahib on its campus for the convenience of its Sikh students and staff.
Foundation stone of Gurudwara Sahib was laid by Sant Baba Isher Singh Ji of Nanaksar on 30th May 1962.
